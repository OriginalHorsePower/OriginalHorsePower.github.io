About Us

Our little corner of the world is all about workin' with our two trusty horses to farm the land and get some timber the old-fashioned way. We call our wagon home, and it's drawn by our strong and steady horses. They ain't just our work partners, they're family. We all work together, and we wouldn't have it any other way.

Services

  • Farming the way it's meant to be

    Our horses help us plow, sow seeds, and tend to the crops. It's honest, earth-friendly work that keeps us connected to Mother Nature.

  • Timber work like the good ol' days

    We work hand in hand (or hoof) with our horses to carefully cut down and haul timber, makin' sure we don't harm the land around us.

  • Take a ride in our horse-drawn wagon

    If you ever want to see how we do things or just have a fun day out, our horse-drawn wagon is ready for a ride through the wild with you.
