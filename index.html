About Original Horse Powered Agriculture

Welcome to Original Horse Powered Agriculture, where we breathe life into time-honored agricultural practices with a touch of modern innovation. Our family-owned and operated company offers a unique and sustainable approach to agriculture and timber extraction, harnessing the power of our two majestic horses as the driving force.

Nestled in the heart of nature, our passionate and skilled team lives and works out of a beautifully crafted horse-drawn wagon, enabling us to immerse ourselves in the environment we are devoted to preserving. Through our intimate connection with the land, we are dedicated to providing eco-friendly agricultural services and traditional timber extraction methods that evoke the spirit of a bygone era.

At Original Horse Powered Agriculture, we are proud to offer:

  • Sustainable agriculture and land management: Our horses plow the fields, sow seeds, and assist in the cultivation of crops, ensuring a natural and environmentally friendly approach to farming.
  • Old-fashioned timber extraction and felling: With precision and care, our team and horses work in harmony to fell and extract timber, maintaining the integrity of the surrounding ecosystem.
  • Custom horse-drawn wagon experiences: Whether you're interested in touring our operation or seeking a unique adventure, our horse-drawn wagon provides a picturesque and memorable experience.

Join us at Original Horse Powered Agriculture as we pay homage to the rich history of working with horses, while embracing a sustainable future for our planet. Experience the wonders of the past with the knowledge of the present, and rediscover the magic of true partnership between humans and horses.
